How does it work?

Create

Start by making a bucket list which includes the things that you want to buy or achieve in your life, by adding items and its description.

Sneak

Sneak into your friend’s bucket to see his gift list and even lock an item from your friend’s bucket without letting him know.

Find Best deals

The item on the list can be used as a medium to direct the user to the best deals simply by hovering on it, and being directed to item providers of the listed dealers through skimlinks.

Gifting moments/Timeline

Capturing the unexpected/surprise moments not just gifting, but also the achievements and share it with the world and cherish it for lifetime.
